## NARRATIVE THESIS

Ra's Al Ghul is a **mentor turned enemy**. He doesn't attack Bruce — he *teaches* him. Then forces Bruce to use those teachings against him.

**The student surpasses the mentor by applying the framework with a different moral compass.**
