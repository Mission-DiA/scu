### EP1: THE FOUNDATION
**Delivery:** Pre-event (cold open, no context)
**Dominant Villain:** Ra's Al Ghul / Ducard
**Film:** Batman Begins (2005)
**Purpose:** Experience the framework being LEARNED

**Five Pivotal Scenes:**
1. **Scene 14:** Blindfolded Training ‚Üí CLARITY ("Always mind your surroundings")
2. **Scene 15-16:** Bat Cave ‚Üí COURAGE ("Master your own fear")
3. **Scene 17:** Execution Refusal ‚Üí COMMITMENT ("The will to act")
4. **Scene 21:** Manor Burns ‚Üí MOVEMENT (Forward is the only direction)
5. **Scene 24:** Train Fight ‚Üí ALL FOUR (Student surpasses mentor)

### EP2: THE TEST
**Delivery:** During event (in-person with simulation)
**Dominant Villain:** Joker
