## Compression Techniques

### 1. MULTI-PANEL SLIDES
For montage/transition scenes, combine 2-3 panels into one slide:

```
Instead of:
- Slide 1: Bruce punches training dummy
- Slide 2: Bruce does pushups
- Slide 3: Bruce meditates

Do:
- Slide 1: Three-panel grid showing training progression
```

### 2. ELIMINATE REDUNDANT ANGLES
If you have:
- Wide shot of monastery
- Medium shot of monastery
- Close shot of monastery entrance

Keep ONE. The establishing shot. Move on.

### 3. MERGE REACTION + ACTION
Instead of:
- Slide 1: Ra's speaks
- Slide 2: Bruce reacts

Do:
- Slide 1: Both in frame, dialogue delivered, reaction visible

### 4. CUT TRANSITIONAL PANELS
