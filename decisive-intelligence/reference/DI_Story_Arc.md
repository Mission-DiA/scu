## THE THESIS

**Decisive Intelligence is a discipline, not a moment.**

Across three episodes, we watch Bruce Wayne:
1. **LEARN** the framework from a mentor (Ra's Al Ghul)
2. **TEST** the framework against chaos (Joker)

Each villain represents a different challenge to decision-making. The four pillars remain constant. The application evolves.
