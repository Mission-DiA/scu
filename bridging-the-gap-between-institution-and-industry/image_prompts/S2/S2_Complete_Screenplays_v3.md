### Core Message

Season 1 was about choosing YOUR path. Season 2 is about what comes next: you don't just GET a career — you BUILD one. A job is something that happens to you. A career is something you construct.

### Episode Structure (v3)

Each episode follows this pattern:
1. **Setup** — Daisy in her world, doing her thing
2. **Confusion** — Coulson introduces the question/challenge
3. **Journey** — Daisy works through it across multiple slides
4. **Action** — Daisy starts applying the lesson
5. **Resolution Beat** — Daisy + Coulson huddle, map confusion → clarity
6. **Hook** — Coulson teases connection to something larger
7. **Watcher Closing** — Cosmic perspective + tease NEXT episode
8. **End Card**

### Critical Production Rule

**ONE DIALOGUE PER SPEAKER PER PANEL**
- Each character speaks MAX once per panel
- Multiple characters CAN speak in same panel (one bubble each)
- NO character speaks twice in same panel
- If dialogue exchange needed → split into more panels or slides
