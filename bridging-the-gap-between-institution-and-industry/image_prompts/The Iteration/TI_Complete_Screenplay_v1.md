```
SAFEHOUSE PROTOCOL — Regular Checkpointing

FREQUENCY: Every 15-20 minutes of active work
           OR at every significant decision
           OR before any context-heavy operation

CHECKPOINT CONTENTS:
1. Current objective (what are we building?)
2. Decisions made this session (with rationale)
3. Current status (where are we?)
4. Blockers (what's stopping us?)
5. Next steps (what's next?)
6. Key context (critical background info)

STORAGE: /01_context/checkpoints/[timestamp].md

RULE: If you haven't checkpointed in 20 minutes, stop and checkpoint.
```
